BTCST
Bitcoin Standard Hashrate Token
Bitcoin Standard Hashrate Token is the bridge between hashrate assets and the DeFi world. Each BTCST is collateralized by 0.1 TH/s of Bitcoin mining power. By staking BTCSTs, holders of the tokens are entitled to Bitcoin and synthetic PoW asset rewards.
